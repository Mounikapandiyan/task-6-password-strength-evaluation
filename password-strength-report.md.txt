 Common Password Attacks

	Brute Force Attack
			A brute force attack tries all possible combinations of characters until the correct password is found. Short and simple passwords can be cracked quickly.

	Dictionary Attack
			A dictionary attack uses a predefined list of common words, names, and leaked passwords to guess credentials. Passwords containing names or common words are highly vulnerable.
